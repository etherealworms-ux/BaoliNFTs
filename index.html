What is BAO LI? +
BAO LI is an 888-piece collection of fully hand-drawn 1/1 NFTs. Unlike traditional generative projects, every single piece is individually crafted by pixel artist @0xmayoo without the use of algorithms, AI assistance, or templates. It’s a tribute to the restless soul of the real-world panda, Bao Li, transitioning from the mountains of Sichuan to the neon-lit urban landscape.
Total Supply? +
The supply is strictly fixed at 888 unique pieces. There will be no expansion, no additional mints, and no dilution of the collection. Each piece is a genuine 1/1 where no two artworks share the same hand strokes.

We prioritize execution over empty promises. Our focus is on delivering high-quality, handcrafted art and fostering a dedicated holder base. Future phases include ecosystem expansion, honorary commissions, and collaborative pieces, with Sentinels receiving priority access to these milestones.
